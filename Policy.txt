Facebook Developer Policy

1. Introduction
This policy outlines the guidelines for developing and managing applications using the Facebook Developer platform. It ensures compliance with Facebook's terms and conditions, protects user data, and fosters ethical usage of the platform.

2. Compliance with Facebook Policies
As a developer, you must comply with the following Facebook policies:

Facebook Platform Terms
Facebook Community Standards
Data Policy
Any other relevant guidelines set forth by Facebook
Failure to comply with these terms may result in your application being disabled or restricted.

3. User Data and Privacy
Your app must comply with all relevant laws regarding data privacy, including GDPR, CCPA, and others. You must ensure the following:

Data Collection: Clearly inform users what data is collected, how it will be used, and how long it will be stored.
User Consent: Obtain explicit user consent before collecting, using, or sharing their data.
Data Sharing: Do not share users' personal data with third parties without their consent.
User Rights: Allow users to access, modify, and delete their personal data.
4. Data Security
You must ensure appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard user data:

Encryption: Implement encryption protocols to protect sensitive data in transit and at rest.
Access Controls: Restrict access to user data to authorized personnel only.
Breach Notification: Notify Facebook and affected users within 72 hours in the event of a data breach.
5. Platform Integrity
To maintain the integrity of Facebook's platform, you must adhere to the following:

App Quality: Ensure your app is free of malware, viruses, or any malicious software.
Spam Prevention: Do not use Facebook's platform for spamming or any other abusive practices.
User Experience: Create apps that provide real value to users, avoiding disruptive ads or misleading content.
6. API Usage

Rate Limits: Follow Facebook’s rate limits and avoid abusing APIs.
Permitted Actions: Only use APIs for permitted purposes as specified in Facebook's API documentation.
Changes to APIs: Monitor any changes to Facebook's API and update your app accordingly.
7. Advertising and Promotions

Transparency: Clearly distinguish between organic content and sponsored content.
Third-Party Ads: Follow Facebook’s guidelines on third-party ads and refrain from deceptive advertising.
8. User Authentication

Use Facebook Login only as intended to authenticate users and respect their privacy settings.
Do not misuse the permissions granted by users during login.
9. Reporting Violations
Report any violations of Facebook’s policies to Facebook’s support team, including suspicious activities or data breaches that you become aware of.

10. Changes to the Policy
This policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in Facebook’s terms or legal requirements. Developers will be notified of significant changes, and continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Consequences of Non-Compliance
Non-compliance with this policy may result in:

Temporary or permanent suspension of app access to Facebook APIs.
Legal action, depending on the severity of the breach.
Reporting to regulatory authorities if the violation pertains to user data misuse or breaches.
